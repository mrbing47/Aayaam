About Us

AAYAAM

Aayaam is the Cultural Club of Computer Science Engineering Department of ABES Institute of Technology. The main motivation behind the formation of this club is to enhance the social values and professional ethics in the students. Various dramatic activities like Spoofs, Mime, Mimicry, Nukkad-Natak, Skit or funny acts and many more are what the club is looking up forward to conduct. Enhancing teamwork, leadership environment, mass exposure for students are some of the main objectives of the club. Moreover promoting those students who are looking forward to work in acting - dramatics as a profession by providing them a platform to show up their skill and talent.

Mimicry

In Mimicry, an artist impersonates a celebrity, generally for entertainment, and makes fun of their personal lives, recent scandals and known behavior patterns.

Mime

Mime involves acting out a story through body motions, without the use of speech.

Nukkad Natak

Nukkad Natak is a form of theatrical performance and presentation in outdoor public spaces without a specific paying audience.
